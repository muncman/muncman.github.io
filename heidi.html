Introduction:

Heidi currently works for America Online where she leads a creative team of 15 designers and photo editors, and manages the design department's daily publishing, commerce, and photography units for the Netscape portal. She brings twelve years of visual design experience to this position, with the last eight years focused on design and promotion of interactive media including portal pages, online advertising, web and stand-alone applications, and consumer-facing web sites. Heidi has a well-rounded creative background, a significant understanding of many technical issues, and a keen understanding of business factors.
