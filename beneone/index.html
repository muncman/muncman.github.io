Better Healthcare Solutions for Real People

Benefits One Consulting is an independent employee benefits and insurance agency that helps clients find the best health insurance program at the most competitive price.

We focus on understanding the unique needs of every client — from individuals and families to small to mid-sized employer groups — so that we can provide personalized service every step of the way. When combined with our experience and strong industry relationships, we are able to provide unique solutions that are best suited to helping our clients protect what matters most — their health and welfare.

Our product focus includes: Medical, Medicare, Dental, Disability and Life insurance.
